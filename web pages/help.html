Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I enroll in a course?
  • What payment methods do you accept?
  • How can I reset my password?

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, follow these steps:
  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Visit the "Courses" section.
  3. Choose the desired course and click on the "Enroll" button.
  4. Complete the payment process.
  5. You are now enrolled in the course and can access the materials.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept payments through credit/debit cards and PayPal. Ensure that your payment method is supported before proceeding with the payment.

How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, you can reset it by following these steps:
  1. Go to the login page.
  2. Click on the "Forgot Password" link.
  3. Enter your email address associated with the account.
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
